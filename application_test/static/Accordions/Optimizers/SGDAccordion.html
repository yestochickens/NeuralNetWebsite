SGD, short for Stochastic Gradient Descent, is the most straightforward optimization algorithm. It directly descends down the calculated gradient.

Typically, SGD allows some modifications from raw gradient descent. Momentum allows for smoother descent with a better chance to end in a global, rather than local minimum. Dampening reduces the current gradient term and allows for more reliance on momentum.

Additionally, descent can be increased through prediction of momentum terms via the Nesterov variant of momentum.
